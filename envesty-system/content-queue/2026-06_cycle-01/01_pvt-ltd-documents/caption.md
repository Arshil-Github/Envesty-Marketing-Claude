12 documents the ROC actually wants before your Pvt Ltd application.

Most first-time founders come in with PAN, Aadhaar, and a photo — and think that's it.

It isn't.

Here's what actually gets checked when your SPICe+ form lands with the RoC officer:

→ DSC (Class 3) — valid and not expired
→ DIN — active for each director
→ Name availability — MCA search + IP India trademark check
→ MOA object clause — cross-referenced against the RoC's restricted activities list
→ Subscriber sheet — properly notarized, not just signed

The MCA portal will accept a form that's missing half of this. The officer won't.
