## Slide 3 — Comparison
**Headline:** What most guides list vs. what RoC-Kanpur actually checks

| Left: What guides tell you | Right: What the officer reviews |
|---|---|
| PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, passport photos. File SPICe+ and wait. | DSC validity, DIN status, name availability on MCA + IP India, MOA object clause vs. restricted activities list, subscriber sheet notarization. |

## Slide 4 — Comparison
**Headline:** The 12 documents — split by who prepares them

| Left: Founder prepares (7) | Right: CS/agent prepares (5) |
|---|---|
