# Slot 01 — Legal Agreements (Education Carousel)
**Cycle:** 2026-06_cycle-02
**Pillar:** Education
**Template flow:** T01_Cover → T02_BigStat → T03_Comparison → T03_Comparison → T04_Quote → T05_CTA

---

## Slide-by-slide brief

**Slide 1 — Cover (T01_Cover)**
Headline: "5 agreements every Indian founder must have."
Subhead: "Most founders discover the gap when a deal falls through."
Tone: Direct, slightly ominous. No stock people imagery. Typography-led layout.

**Slide 2 — BigStat (T02_BigStat)**
Stat: "1 missing clause"
Subhead: "That's all it takes for a co-founder dispute to freeze your company — bank accounts, domain, everything locked."
Note: avoids a fabricated survey number. The "1 missing clause" is the real mechanic — verifiable.

**Slide 3 — Comparison (T03_Comparison): Co-Founder Agreement**
Left (Without it): Partner keeps 50% equity after walking away — company paralysed.
Right (With it): Reverse vesting + buy-back formula + dispute mechanism written before there's a dispute.
Design note: Make the LEFT side feel viscerally real. This is the most share-triggering card.

**Slide 4 — Comparison (T03_Comparison): IP Assignment**
Left: Domain, brand, codebase in a personal name — they leave, your brand has no legal owner.
Right: Everything assigned to the company day 1, including future employee/vendor output.
Design note: Concrete scenario on the left ("they leave"). No abstraction.

**Slide 5 — Quote/List (T04_Quote)**
The remaining 3 agreements as a tight list inside the quote card.
Attribution: "Envesty, est. Feb 2025" — using "est." is honest tenure framing per brand kit.

**Slide 6 — CTA (T05_CTA)**
Headline: "Comment AGREEMENTS below."
Body: "I'll DM you the checklist we walk every new client through — all 5 documents with the specific clauses to include."
Mirrors the high-engagement comment-to-DM mechanic: top-performing pattern in the reel dataset (@chintan_gelot, 574K views; @casarthakahuja, 541K views; both use this exact mechanic).

## Reel analysis pattern applied
Structured after @casarthakahuja's "5 legal documents every founder must have" (541K views, 12K shares, 41K shares across their legal content).
Format: fear hook → stat that makes it real → before/after per document → list of remaining items → comment-bait CTA.
That is the highest-share pattern in the analysed dataset for educational content in the Indian founder niche.
