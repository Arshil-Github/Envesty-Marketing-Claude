My first company raised ₹10 Cr at a ₹20 Cr valuation.

It still didn't make it to profitability.

I don't say that for sympathy. I say it because the three gaps that hurt us are the same ones I see in almost every founder we talk to at Envesty.

Gap 1 — Legal infrastructure
We used a template MOA. Skipped the co-founder agreement. No IP assignment for the domain or codebase. Found the gaps 18 months in, when it was expensive to fix.

Gap 2 — Brand credibility
We launched with inflated claims. Felt professional. Cost us trust with the investors and clients who actually looked us up.

Gap 3 — Vendor fragmentation
4 vendors, 4 contracts, nobody owning the outcome. Every handoff was a delay. Every delay had someone else to blame.

Envesty is the answer I wish we'd had in 2016.
