## Positioning

**One-liner:** Envesty is the virtual co-founder for first-time Indian founders — legal, product, brand, and growth orchestrated as one accountable team.

**Promise:** Replace fragmented agencies and freelance vendors with one execution layer that gets your company started and growing.

**Proof angle:** Integrated bundle (Legal + Technology + Marketing + Consultation) under one team, with transparent pricing and timelines. The unfair advantage is **radical transparency** — published prices, published timelines, real names, real reviews — at a moment when the loudest incumbents (RegisterKaro etc.) are taking heat for aggressive upselling.

## Audience

- **Primary ICP:** First-time founders and solo entrepreneurs in India (gravity: Delhi-NCR / Noida / UP) registering a Pvt Ltd or LLP, navigating GST/compliance for the first time, and needing tech + marketing without juggling 4 vendors.
- **Secondary:** Early-stage Indian startups (0–24 months old) outgrowing freelance arrangements and wanting one accountable team.
- **Tertiary:** Growing MSMEs that need compliance + digital + AI execution but lack in-house staff.
- **Disqualified:** Late-stage / Series-B+ companies (Envesty isn't a fit), companies needing only a single deep service (use a specialist), founders shopping purely on lowest-price incorporation (the commoditized ₹999 segment).

## Voice & tone

- **Adjectives:** founder-empathetic, plainspoken, structured, accountable, slightly contrarian about the "ecosystem theatre" of the Indian startup scene.
- **Forbidden phrases / claims:**
  - "5+ years of expertise," "100+/200+ businesses served," "98% satisfaction," or any tenure/scale claim the company has not earned. We are <18 months old.
  - Anything implying funded scale, large team, or third-party endorsement we do not have.
  - Generic startup vocabulary (synergy, leverage, unlock, ecosystem play, disrupting the space) used without specifics.
  - "Trust us" — we earn trust by showing pricing, timelines, and names.
- **Sample sentence (good):** "Pvt Ltd incorporation in India takes 12–15 working days if the DSC, name reservation, and MOA/AOA are sequenced right. Here is what each step actually costs."
- **Sample sentence (bad):** "With 5+ years of expertise serving 100+ businesses, Envesty delivers world-class compliance solutions."

## Hook formulas

Each formula has one example. Cycle generation should sample from these — not invent new ones — and the examples are starting points, not lines to reuse verbatim.

1. **Specific number + plain noun** — "12 documents the ROC actually wants before your Pvt Ltd application."
2. **Counter-narrative** — "₹999 incorporation isn't cheap. Here's the bill nobody quotes."
3. **Founder vulnerability** — "My first company raised $1.5M and didn't make it. Here's what I'd do on day 1 now."
4. **Before/after artefact** — "Compliance checklist a Noida founder sent us vs. what we shipped back."
5. **Insider mechanic** — "How ROC-Kanpur actually reads your MOA — and the 3 lines that get it rejected."

## Distribution & cadence

- **Primary channel:** **Founder-led LinkedIn.** Both Sushant and Mohit post 3–5×/week as individuals. Per Refine Labs, personal profiles drive ~2.75× impressions and ~5× engagement vs. company pages — so the carousels generated by this system are designed for *personal* feeds first, company page second.
- **Secondary:** Instagram @envesty.in and the company LinkedIn page (repurpose, don't originate).
- **Tertiary:** Email newsletter (once the list crosses 100 opted-in founders).

## Guardrails (read before every cycle)

1. **No fabricated proof.** Stats, client counts, testimonials, "rated 5 stars," "trusted by X." If we can't name the source on demand, it doesn't ship.
2. **Use real names.** If a slide attributes a quote, it's a real person who consented.
3. **Don't recycle the dossier's competitor intel as direct attack ads.** Counter-position by *being* transparent, not by naming and shaming.
4. **DPDP Act caution.** Don't write copy that implies we collect or resell client data, and the dossier flag re: envesty.com privacy policy needs the founders to confirm it's been remediated before any "we protect your data" content runs.
5. **Tenure honesty.** When the date matters, say "since Feb 2025" or "in our first year." Never imply legacy.
